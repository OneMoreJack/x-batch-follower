# Privacy Policy for One-Click Follower for X

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how "One-Click Follower for X" handles your data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**"One-Click Follower for X" does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to external servers.**

*   **Account Discovery**: The extension processes the text content of the visible webpage to identify X (Twitter) handles. This processing happens entirely within your browser.
*   **Following Action**: When you click "Follow All," the extension automates the clicking of "Follow" buttons on X.com on your behalf.
*   **Local Storage**: The extension may use Chrome's local storage to save your settings or temporary lists of found accounts. This data remains on your local machine and is never shared with us or any third party.

## 2. Permissions Justification

To provide its core functionality, the extension requires the following permissions:

*   **`scripting` & `activeTab`**: Needed to safely identify X handles on the page you are currently viewing.
*   **`storage`**: Used to remember your preferences locally.
*   **Host Permissions (`https://x.com/*`, `https://twitter.com/*`)**: Required to perform the following actions on your behalf when you are on the X platform.

## 3. Data Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Your data never leaves your device except to interact directly with the X platform as intended by the extension's features.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
